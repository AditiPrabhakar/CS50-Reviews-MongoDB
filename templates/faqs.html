    FAQs

    What is this project about?

    This project is a platform for CS50 students to share their feedback, rate the course, and explore reviews from others. It helps improve the learning experience for everyone involved.

    How can I submit a review?

    Simply navigate to the Create page, fill in your thoughts about the course, and hit "Submit." Your review will be added for others to see!

    Where can I see the reviews submitted by others?

    Visit the Reviews page to explore all the feedback shared by fellow students. You’ll find comments, ratings, and more!

    Can I edit or delete my review after submitting it?

    Currently, we don’t support editing or deleting reviews. Make sure to double-check your review before submitting it!

    Is this platform officially affiliated with CS50?

    No, this is a student-created project inspired by the CS50 course. It is not officially affiliated with or endorsed by Harvard or the CS50 team.

    Who can leave a review?

    Anyone who has taken the CS50 course or is currently enrolled can leave a review.

    Can I ask questions about CS50 here?

    No, this platform is purely for sharing reviews and feedback. For questions, check out CS50 official FAQs or post a question at CS50 Official Communities.

    Can I rate specific sections of the course?

    At this time, reviews cover the course as a whole. We may add section-specific ratings in the future!
